Our Story

A quiet rebellion against noisy coffee chains

Born from a whisper

Hush Cafe started in 2024 with a simple belief: coffee tastes better when you can hear yourself think. Founders Aarav & Meera wanted a space where introverts, readers, remote workers, and dreamers could enjoy exceptional coffee without the chaos.

We roast our beans in small batches, partner directly with farmers in Chikmagalur, and keep our music at 65 decibels (quiet enough for a conversation, loud enough to hide awkward silences).

Come for the pour-over. Stay for the peace.
